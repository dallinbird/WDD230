Site Plan: Lewiston Chamber of Commerce

Site Name: Lewiston Chamber of Commerce

I chose this name because I feel that having a more intricate name would detract from the purpose of the organization. A simple name like this gets to the main point of what the organization is, helps users understand who it is for, and makes it easier for members to direct potential members to the site.

Purpose:

To advocate for the business interests of Lewiston and grow small businesses.

Political Intrest Lobbying

The lewiston Chamber of Commerce advocates the intrest of businesses based out of Lewiston to the state legislative bodies through lobbying and campain donations to potential polititons that promise changes that benefit the business owners of Lewiston.

Business Networking

The Lewiston Chamber of Commerce wishes to see business connections within lewiston streaghthen and provides man means to streagthen local business cooperation. From dinners and webinars to orchestrating meetings between business owners that could mutually benefit from eachothers cooperation.
